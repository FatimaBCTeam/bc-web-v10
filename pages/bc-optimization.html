Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

How is this different from a BC Rescue Audit? +

The BC Rescue Audit is a pure diagnostic — we find what's broken and deliver a prioritized fix plan. BC Optimization is the hands-on work of actually implementing improvements in your live environment. They're complementary: many clients start with an audit to understand the full picture, then move into an optimization sprint to execute on the findings. If you already know where the problems are and just need them fixed, you can skip straight to optimization.

How long does an optimization engagement take? +

Scope drives timeline. A focused engagement — fixing a specific reporting problem or connecting a single integration — can be completed in 2–4 weeks. A broader optimization across multiple areas (reporting, workflows, integrations, and data cleanup) typically runs 6–12 weeks. We always sequence work by ROI, so you see measurable improvements in the first few weeks — not just at the end of the engagement. Before we start, you'll have a clear scope, timeline, and deliverable list in writing.

Do we need to stop or pause operations during optimization? +

No. We work around your business — not the other way around. Changes are tested in a sandbox when required and deployed during off-peak windows. We plan around your month-end close cycles, busy seasons, and operational constraints. The vast majority of clients run their entire optimization engagement while their team continues normal day-to-day operations in BC — with zero disruption to live transactions or posting.

What's the typical ROI? +

Results vary by scope, but the consistent patterns we see: finance teams save 6–12 hours per week on manual reporting, data manipulation, and workaround management. Month-end close time typically drops 30–60%. Automated workflows eliminate recurring manual tasks worth thousands of dollars per month in staff time. Real-time dashboards replace spreadsheet processes that previously consumed entire days. Most clients recover the full cost of their optimization engagement within 60–90 days — measured in staff time saved alone, before any revenue or compliance improvements are counted.
