Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

How quickly can RAP be deployed? +

RAP goes live in a single day. We install it directly into your existing BC environment from Microsoft AppSource, configure your customer groups and statement templates, and your first automated statements can go out within 24 hours. There's no new infrastructure, no migration, and no downtime.

Do we need to be on Business Central already? +

RAP is built natively for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central and Dynamics NAV. If you're on QuickBooks, Sage, or another system, we can help — BC Team also handles full BC migrations, and many companies make the move specifically to unlock AR automation capabilities like RAP.

How is RAP different from BC's built-in reminders? +

BC's built-in reminder functionality is basic — it requires manual triggering, doesn't attach original invoices, and has no collections workflow or cash flow forecasting. RAP is a full AR automation platform: automated scheduling, intelligent escalation sequences, real-time cash flow forecasting, dispute tracking, and a collector priority queue.

What's included in onboarding? +

Taylor personally onboards every RAP customer. This includes installation, configuration of your customer tiers and statement templates, a walkthrough of the collections dashboard with your AR team, and a follow-up call at the 30-day mark to review results and fine-tune settings.

Is there a contract or can we cancel anytime? +

RAP is month-to-month. No long-term contracts. Annual billing is available at a discount. If you cancel, your BC environment returns to its previous state — RAP doesn't modify your core BC configuration.
