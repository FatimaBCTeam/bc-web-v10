RAP — AR Automation

Receivables Automation Platform (RAP) is BC Team's proprietary AR automation product — built natively inside Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. It's the only AR automation tool that lives directly in your existing BC environment, with no new system to learn and no separate login required.

RAP automates your entire accounts receivable collections process: professional statements with original invoices automatically attached, intelligent escalation sequences by aging bucket, real-time cash flow forecasting from your live AR pipeline, dispute and promise-to-pay tracking, and a daily priority queue for your collectors.
