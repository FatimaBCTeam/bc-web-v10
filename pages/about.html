Background

From the partner channel to building something better.

Taylor McEachnie spent the formative years of his career inside Microsoft Business Central partner firms — the kind of large, multi-practice organizations that sell BC implementations by the hundreds but often deliver them by the template. He learned the software deeply. He also learned exactly what those firms consistently got wrong.

After 19+ years and more than 140 Business Central projects across Canada and the United States, Taylor founded BC Team for one reason: to build the firm he wished existed when he was in the partner channel. A firm that treated BC not as a product to sell but as a foundation to get right — every single time.

BC Team is deliberately product-led. Taylor built RAP — the Receivables Automation Platform — because no existing AR tool was native to BC. Every BC Team engagement reflects that philosophy.

"Set up Business Central wrong from the start and everything downstream pays the price — the chart of accounts, the posting groups, the dimension structure. Every bad decision compounds for years. We've spent 19 years cleaning up those mistakes. We'd rather you not make them." — Taylor McEachnie, President & CEO, BC Team
