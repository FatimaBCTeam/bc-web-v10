Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

What exactly do we get from the audit? +

You receive a full written diagnostic report covering every identified issue in your BC environment, a root cause analysis explaining why each problem exists, a prioritized fix plan with effort estimates and ROI calculations for each item, and a phased implementation roadmap with week-by-week sequencing. We also walk through every finding with you live on a debrief call — nothing is left unclear or buried in the document.

How long does the audit take? +

The Quick Audit is delivered in 5 business days. The Full Deep-Dive Audit is completed within 10 business days. Both timelines start from the day we receive read access to your BC environment and complete a scoping call with your team. We don't drag it out — you have a business to run, and a prolonged diagnostic doesn't serve anyone.

Do you fix the issues, or just diagnose them? +

The Rescue Audit is a pure diagnostic engagement — you get a complete, actionable picture of what's wrong and exactly how to fix it. Many clients then engage BC Team to implement the recommendations through our BC Optimization service, but there's absolutely no obligation. You own the report and can execute with any partner or internal team. That said, most clients find the transition natural — the roadmap is already written, and you already trust the analysis.

What if we're mid-implementation when we book? +

That's often the best time to audit. A mid-implementation rescue can prevent months of costly rework by identifying configuration errors, missing requirements, and process misalignments before they get permanently baked in. We've helped companies recover from implementations heading toward failure — sometimes with as little as three weeks left before planned go-live. If something feels off about your current implementation, book the call now. Early is always better than late.
